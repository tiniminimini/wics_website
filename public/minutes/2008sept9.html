What we are doing this year
  • Lounge Redecorating Party: Spruce up the lounge. Ideas Welcome. Deocrate with XKCD comics? Yes!
  • Weekly WiCS Mixer: Regular time where people meet in the lounge to hang out and eat snacks. The main goal of which would be to foster bonding and give people a place to feel like they belong in the cs building.
  • Mentor Program: Assigning specific mentors has failed in the past. New program will be more informal. We will either assign freshmen to a pool of WiCS mentors or having a set time(s) where freshmen and upperclassmen could meet and upperclassmen could share advice.
  • Bagels: We are no longer selling bagels.
  • CS WIN: Like the Women in Industry Network only without a Chem Eng making a speech for 1.5 hours and with more CS people
